Our Delivery Process & Milestones

Last Updated: May 2026 • Author: Logistics Team • Category: Work Process

At KCRACKER, we maintain a transparent, highly structured milestone process for all reverse engineering and decryption projects. This guide outlines exactly what you can expect from the moment you submit a protected file to final project handover.

The 5-Stage Delivery Pipeline

We break down our operational workflow into five distinct phases, ensuring absolute clarity, safety, and validation.

1. Intake & Technical Scan (Free)

Your project begins when you submit your protected files directly to our analyst on Telegram.

  • We run immediate automated static analysis to identify packer, compiler, and obfuscator layers (e.g., PyArmor 7/8/9, Nuitka, Cython, PyInstaller).
  • We verify the integrity of the binary file and test it inside an isolated, non-networked testing sandbox.
  • Timeline: Completed within 2 hours of file receipt.

2. Feasibility Report & Quote (Free)

Once the scan is complete, we issue a structured project proposal. This contains:

  • Our feasibility assessment (confirming whether a complete source reconstruction is possible).
  • A fixed pricing quote based on the complexity of the protection structures.
  • An estimated delivery date (usually 24 to 48 hours).
  • No Obligation: You are free to decline the quote; no charges apply for feasibility scanning.

3. Sandboxed Analysis & Recovery

Upon your approval, we proceed with active reverse engineering:

  • We employ dynamic hook pipelines to capture decrypted bytecode objects in memory.
  • Our internal AST reconstruction tools reconstruct syntax trees and repair stripped headers.
  • If anti-tamper or custom VM layers are active, our analysts perform manual debugging in IDA Pro to bypass the restrictions.

4. Verification Milestone (Proof of Success)

Before any payment is requested, we compile indisputable proof of successful recovery:

  • Interactive Video: A high-definition recording demonstrating the recovered script running flawlessly.
  • Structure Map: A complete screenshot of the recovered project’s file and module tree layout.
  • Sample Snippets: 20–30 lines of any specific, non-critical sub-function requested by you to prove code authenticity.

5. Secure Handoff & Archive Purge

Once you review and approve the verification proof:

  • You submit the payment using one of our supported cryptocurrencies (USDT, LTC, BTC, or TRX).
  • We immediately deliver the full ZIP archive containing the recovered files via an encrypted, password-protected transfer link.
  • We remain available for 7 days post-delivery to provide complimentary integration support.
  • On the 7th day, we execute a secure sanitization script, permanently purging all submitted files and source code from our local drives.

General Terms & Guarantees

  • No Deobfuscation, No Fee: If we accept your project but encounter unexpected compiler structures that prevent complete code recovery, you owe nothing.
  • Post-Delivery Bug Fixes: If you find an extracted submodule is missing imports or experiences runtime errors due to unpacking offsets within 7 days of handoff, we will repair it free of charge.
